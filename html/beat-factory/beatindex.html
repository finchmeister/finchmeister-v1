The Beat Factory

A place where beats come to die...

Since discovering GarageBand, I've become a music producer, laying down dope-ass beats and face melting guitar on some of my favourite tracks.

You'll only find hurried and under produced songs at The Beat Factory right now, but like the rest of this site, it's in development, so stay tuned for, err..., more tunes!

Welcome to The Beat Factory, enjoy.

Panacea. Because jazz is cool.

One day I'd like to play jazz properly, but playing jazz properly is hard, so this will have to do. If you want to hear true jazzy genius, check out the original by Greyboy here. Look out for the saxophone solo in the middle. It's real nice.

Under Your Spell. A cover from the Drive soundtrack.

The first song to drop from The Beat Factory, Under Your Spell started life as an irritating fumbling mess on the house piano. The next step, of course, was to produce the whole thing in GarageBand and slap a dreamy guitar solo in the middle - this is the story of The Beat Factory. If you can sing this, get in touch.
