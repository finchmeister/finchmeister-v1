The Beat Factory

Blending a raw, uncut bootleg quality with unambitious and underdeveloped source material, The Beat Factory is where I’ve slapped together recently recorded material made in the 'studio'. I have high hopes for The Beat Factory, with more time and imagination, one day it will become great.

Just in case you didn't notice, the theme took inspiration from an 80’s Miami Vice, the music less so.

Poker Hand Simulator

How good is this hand, is it worth playing? I know that bad beat was bad, but how bad? Is nine ten suited really as good as I think it is?... All questions and more that can be answered by the Poker Hand Simulator.

It calculates the odds of each player winning the hand by running a thousand simulations from the given situation. Go get involved.
